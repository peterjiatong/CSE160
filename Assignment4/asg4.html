I reused most part of my code from Assignment 3, but instead of generate a maze, I use only a Cube and a Sphere for this one

Feature:

1. Have at least one cube and Created a sphere

2. Button to visualize normals with color

3. 3 types of light added (ambient, diffuse and specular), and a visual marker shows the location of the light

4. A user interface button to turn on and off lighting

5. The point light moves around the world over time, you can use slider to change its location, and use button to make the point light stop moving

Camera movement:

Use W, A, S, D for move forward, backward, left and right

Use Q or E to rotate camera to left or right, use Shift & Q or Shift & E to rotate your camera 90 degree to left or right

Or you can also rotate camera using mouse
